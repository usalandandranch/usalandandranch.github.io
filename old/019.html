Seed Cleaning Business in Seymour, TX - $200,000

 
Play
Pause
 
Long time seed cleaning business in Seymour Texas is for sale. The owners started this business in the 1970s and have continually expanded and built the business. The owners do business in a 50 mile radius of their warehouse in Seymour and can handle the business with 3 – 6 workers. Company is now for sale of all the equipment and business.

This company will go into the country with a vac-u-later and seed cleaner and trucks, and this cleaning business is completely mobile. Vac-u-later will remove the seed from the bins and put them through the cleaner while the grain bins are being cleaned. The clean seed is then replaced into the bins and ready for planting. This company will clean 120,000 – 150,000 bushels of wheat annually and their gross receipts average over $130,000 annually for a business that runs from May to November. Sellers estimate their net margin to be 55% or higher.

This company is for sale at $200,000 and this price is primarily equipment:
  • Vac-u-later
  • Seed Cleaner 
  • Freightliners with trailers (2) 
  • Pickups with flatbed (2) 
  • 1 ton with flatbed 
  • 3 augers


With a business that nets around $70,000+ a year and equipment that makes up about 60% of the list price this company is priced to sell.


Listed exclusively at $200,000 with Ag Brokers, Ltd.
