Roedtan

List #: 1411212

Price: $1,750,000 USD /  R 17.5mill 

This price excludes VAT. The Selling Price is based on several factors pertaining to recent farm sales in the area as well as the size of the subject property.

Size: 2,907 acres $590 per ac / ±1188 hectares with two title deeds – bordering each other with no servitudes over the property.

Locality: The Farm is situated in the Springbok flats in close proximity to Roedtan (past Naboomspruit on route Marble Hall). This property  is ±200 km North, North East of Pretoria. Oliver Tambo International (previously known as Johannesburg International) Airport is only 280 km away by tarred road and 35 minutes away by chartered aircraft. 

Registration Detail: The farm consists of two title deeds which is registered in a Company and is VAT registered.
Province: Limpopo Province
Servitude:  No road subdivides over any portion of this farm.
Ownership: The property can be bought out of the Company

1 X Wooden house which have 2 Bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, air-conditioning, a kitchen/lounge area with built in fireplace/braai.
2 x Log Chalets 50m² each which has 1 bedroom and 1 bathroom each as well as equipped with air-conditioning. 
1 x 3 Bedroom brick house for farm manager 
1 x Double storey tree house (Maroela tree)
Spacious thatch roof lapa (100m²) with kitchen, bathroom and storeroom. 
Boma with fireplace for braai and socializing.
Swimming pool & Jacuzzi
3 x Permanently parked Caravans under roof equipped with power and light.
Caravan stands with power points and 1 wooden stand for caravan with basin and bathroom with hot water.
Ablution facilities consisting of 2 X bathrooms for caravan stands with shower/basin/toilet with hot water.
Basic Slaughtering facilities
Workers accommodation
Shed and other outbuildings

Game Farm Detail: This is one Superb Game Farm in the extend of ±1188 Hectares. This property offer you an intimate owner-run bush manor all to yourself… with  a swimming pool to enjoy our African Sunsets, take a gentle safari and view that game up close… this is a gorgeous property suitable for a family.
Roads: The farm roads give good access to all sections of the farm and are maintained with its own necessary equipment by the ground staff. The farm has several kilometers of well-maintained roads plus plenty of additional trails which are also well maintained.

Game Fence: Fully game fenced accordingly to the requirements of the Dept. Nature Conservation and the majority of the game fence is 2.4m high with 21 strands. This fence is regular patrolled by ground staff.

Water: There are two earth dams – the smaller one is in close proximity to the lodge area. There are 4 bore holes from which only 2 boreholes are equipped with submersible pumps (7.5kw en 3.75kw).  This is a region experiences an average rainfall of 650 - 850 mm per annum. There is a network of pipes and cribs for water supply to all game all over the farm.

Game: The Farm is extremely well stocked but also well balanced with a large variety of game. This region well known as the heartbeat of the sweetvelt grazing and breeding mecca, there are some of the largest trophy game in South Africa. There is a "Step on" tick control for the game on the farm. The following game numbers are very conservative and only estimated numbers: 
Impala ±100,          Kudu ±30,             Eland ±3,          Gemsbuck ±19,         Waterbuck ±10,      
R/H Beest ±25,        Blesbuck ±35,        Giraffe±19,
Also occurring on the property is the following: Steenbuck, Bushbuck, Duiker, Warthog, Bushpig, Caracal, African Civet, Large & small Genet, Porcupine, Ant bear, Pangolin, Velvet monkey, Yellow mongoose, Honey badger, Lesser bush baby, Slender mongoose,  Tree squirrel etc.
