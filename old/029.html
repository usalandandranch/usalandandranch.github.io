Naboomspruit

List #: 1411213

Price: $1,980,000 USD /  R 19.8mill 

This price excludes VAT. The Selling Price is based on several factors pertaining to recent farm sales in the area as well as the size of the subject property.

Size: ±2,607 acres $760 per ac / ±1043 hectares.

Locality: The Farm is situated in the Waterberg area just past Naboomspruit (now Mookgopong) which is ±200 km North, North East of Pretoria. Oliver Thambo International (previously knows as Johannesburg International) Airport is only 220 km away by tarred road and 35 minutes away by chartered aircraft. Trips to the Entabeni Big 5 Game Reserve and world renown Golf Course is all within a few minutes’ drive from the farm, enable spectacular game viewing of the Big 5, which can be seen up close. 

Registration Detail: The farm consists of several title deeds which is registered in a Close Corporation and is VAT registered.
Province: Limpopo Province Servitude's:  There is a private road that runs over a small portion of the farm.

Other: The property has a nice managers house, store room, slaughtering room with cold room and labour accommodation.

Game Farm Detail: This is one Superb Game Farm 

Game Farm in extend of ±2706 acres 
Situated in a malaria free area, this Jewel of a game farm is ecologically superb with a rich mix of bushveld attractive rock formation at the far back of the farm and which covers a mere 5% of the total surface of the farm while the rest is relative flat with beautiful plains. The entrance gate promise an enjoyable experience to come unto this game farm.

Roads: The farm roads give good access to all sections of the farm. The farm has several kilometres of roads plus plenty of additional trails which is set out for quad trails.

Game Fence: Fully game fenced accordingly to the requirements of the Dept. Nature Conservation. Game fences are regular patrolled by ground staff. Only a small area has no game fencing due to a servitude and sheer cliffs.


Water: There is 3 earth dams and a perennial sprout that runs through the farm and is ample water suitable for human and animal consumption - Fish Eagles regularly visits this dam. Water is also obtained from 2 boreholes and cement reservoir feeding game drinking troughs which are widely spread out on the farm. 


Flora: There is a great variety grasses and trees. Herewith is a short list of just a few of the many trees:
Trees are not only functional, but also aesthetically pleasing to the eye, as can be seen from pictures. A wide variety of indigenous trees cover these beautiful area forming a breathtaking tranquil landscape and being an important part of animal, bird and insect nutrition.

Fauna: The Farm is extremely well stocked but also well balanced with a large variety of game. This region, being the centre of the South African breeding heartbeat, and well known between local game breeders, has some of the largest game breeders in the province.

Topography: Flat Serengeti Alike plains. Rolling and broken - very scenic with an almost sandy type of loam soil. There is an abundant of roads on the farm and all are in a good general condition. This beautiful farm has it all – 95% flat with a slight slope which consists of plains, a river, parts is lovely bush, parts is open areas,- all in one. This is one of the most “all purpose” farms in the Waterberg. You can do allot on a farm if you have water. The area is well known for its sublime views over the waterberg mountains with an abundance of bushveld indigenous trees together with a combination of rolling plains, perennial rivers and typical views over the Waterberg foothills and its surrounding ravines. This quality game farm is well situated in proximity to Pretoria (1H30  drive). If you are serious about buying a dream game farm and lifestyle then this is the one!!
