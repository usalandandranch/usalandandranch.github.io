Thabazimbi

List #: 1510012

Price: $1,030,000 USD /  R 14.3mill 

This price excludes VAT. The Selling Price is based on several factors pertaining to recent farm sales in the area as well as the size of the subject property.

Size:  2,750 acres / ±1113 hectares

Location: The Farm is situated North-West of Thabazimbi which is ±265 km from Pretoria in the Limpopo Province of South Africa. Oliver Tambo International (previously known as Johannesburg International) Airport is only 40 minutes away by chartered aircraft.

Registration Detail: The farm consists of one title deed which is registered in a Trust which is VAT registered.
Province: Limpopo Province
Servitude’s:  No road subdivide any portion of the farm.  

Developments: The property is an inner farm which make it a very lucrative proposition. The property have 3 phase Eskom. 
The Main house is a thatch roof house consisting of 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, kitchen and lounge area. There is an additional 2 bedrooms outside with 2 bathrooms as well as an Rounded Chalets with 1 bedroom + bathroom.

There is a 14 bed Hunters camp and there is a good boma and BBQ area with a swimming pool. 

Additional constructions: These additional constructions are:
There is an enormous  store room, and two small feed storage rooms. 
There are 4 Labour rooms.
There a 3 Bow-blinds (for bow hunting)  
There are two cold rooms and two slaughtering rooms. 

Game: This is one Superb Game Farm, Breeding and Hunting farm for the more discerned buyer that knows what he wants in life.
 
Game Farm in extend of ±1113Hectares
Situated in a malaria free area, this Jewel of a game farm is ecologically superb with a rich mix of sweet bushveld attractive flora and a 100% flat typography. The entrance gate is constructed on the inner road and promise an enjoyable experience to come to this game farm…far from the city lights and pollution.

Roads
The farm roads give good access to all sections of the farm. The farm has several kilometres of roads plus plenty of additional trails. 

Game Fence
Fully game fenced accordingly to the requirements of the Dept. Nature Conservation. Game fences are regular patrolled by ground staff. 

The game breeding camps are as follows:
3 x ±25ha
1 x ±15ha
1 x ±10ha 

Water
The property has 2 equipped boreholes, 3 earth dams and 3 water points for game and 4 cement dams.

Flora
There is a great variety grasses and trees typical associated with the sweetveld area. These trees are not only functional, but also aesthetically pleasing to the eye, as can be seen from pictures. A wide variety of indigenous trees cover this beautiful area forming a breath-taking tranquil landscape and being an important part of animal, bird and insect nutrition.
 
Fauna
The Farm is extremely well stocked but also well balanced with a large variety of game. This region, being the centre of the South African hunting heartbeat, and well known between local as well as overseas hunters, has some of the largest trophy game in South Africa. 

Mammals:
Impala ±300,                       Kudu ±50,                   B/W Beest ±100,
Eland ±20,                          Zebra ±30,                   Waterbuck ±50,   
Giraffe 2
Other wildlife that commonly occure on the property is Steenbuck, Duiker, Warthog, Bushpig, Porcupine, Pangolin, Vervet monkey, Yellow mongoose, Honey badger, Hedgehog, Lesser bush baby, Tree squirrel etc. This farm has exceptional nocturnal life. 

Game in the Breeding camps are not included in the selling price but offers will be looked at:
1 x Black Impala ram + 20 Split ewes + 15 normal Impala ewes.
1 x Nyala bull + 10 ewes. 
1 x 58 inch Kudu bull + 10 Kudu cows.
20 Nyala + 3 Sable bulls (they walk on the open property but also NOT included in the selling price)

Impression: This property can be described as Africa's own piece of heaven with clear star light & spectacular sunsets. The game farm is isolated from city life and is a true African game farm offering bush experience with all the luxurious you would need. This is a sweetveld game ranch running currently as a both a Hunting and Game Breeding Ranch where guests enjoy the luxury and splendour of the African Bush. The farm will be sold as a going concern - lock, stock and barrel (Inventory list available to serious buyers). This property is ideal for someone who is looking for a well developed game farm with everything already in its place.
