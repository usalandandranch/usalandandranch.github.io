Auctions

USA Land and Ranch, LLC, in cooperation with its subsidiary organization, Caprock Auction Group, offers a variety of services to fit your auction needs. We offer on-site, off-site, live and online only auctions. Our professional staff of auctioneers, ringmen and bid callers have experience with all manner of auction styles and settings. If you are seeking an auction house through which to sell acreage, residential or commercial properties, estate liquidations, business liquidations, consignment property or are seeking a benefit auction, we can work with you. Please contact us at auctions@usalandandranch.com OR at 806-680-1YEP for further information.
