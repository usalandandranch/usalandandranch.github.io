Game Farm Detail: This is one Superb Game Farm to be sold as a going concern inclusive of all the lodge equipment, furniture, farm vehicles, farm equipment and tools.

Game Farm in extend of 1221.7201 Hectares: Situated in a malaria free area, this Jewel of a game farm is ecologically superb with a rich mix of bushveld sweetveld areas and open savannah areas. Attractive koppies and mountains in the backdrop give this property a real Africa feel. The entrance gate is bordering a well maintained gravel road and promise and enjoyable experience to come unto this farm.

Roads: The dirt roads give good access to all sections of the farm and are maintained with its own necessary equipment by the ground staff. The farm has sufficient and well maintained roads plus plenty of additional trails which are also well maintained.

Game Fence: Fully game fenced accordingly to the requirements of the Dept. Nature Conservation. There are 2 game breeding areas specially fenced off and is also jackal proof and consist of electrified game fencing.

Water: The Water reticulation on this farm is above average as all areas of the farm are covered in terms of a supply point per area. There are several earth dams on this farm as well as some water holes where water is periodically pumped into them from the boreholes. The farm has ample water suitable for human and animal consumption.

Water from is also obtained from the Magalies Water Scheme and there are 4 boreholes on the farm from which 2 equipped, strategically placed to ensure the water troughs and Lodge have water in abundance.

The water holes are well spread throughout the farm, which avoids concentrated damage to the vegetation, by the game. Note: The Game Farm have more water than what they can consume.
