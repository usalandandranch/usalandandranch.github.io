About Us

USA Land and Ranch is a realty and auction house based in Amarillo, TX and serves the surrounding Texas Panahandle. Our team of skilled professionals understand that searching for and finding the right property can be stressful. But the process has never been easier than with the assistance of our dedicated staff, user-friendly site, and our committment to make your real estate experience a comfortable one. USA Land and Ranch Realty is ready to assist you, whether buying or selling, with all of our resources to locate and identity the ideal property to match your needs. Or if selling, to get your property listed and in front of the right prospective buyers. Our staff will work hard to make this an enjoyable and memorable experience for you.

Our expert real estate agents will compare and complete the task for you with skilled knowledge and experience, while simultaneously in the event of investment buying offer management service after purchase.

Mission and Goals

We pledge to conduct business at the highest level of integrity, while employing creativity and determination to strive for client satisfaction.

To engage in ecologically sensible land sales and development practices while being economically responsible to investment buyers and landowners.

To earn every client and fellow land professional's respect and lifetime friendship.

To help landowners and investment buyers preserve agricultural and recreational land indefinitely.

To practice Real Estate with conscience to benefit the long term good of the public, clients, and their neighbors.

To preserve the quality of rural life in Texas by discouraging small tract subdivisions.

Consulting, Planning & Management

Consulting, Planning & Management

Having been directly involved with very many properties being developed and remodeled for resale and improving the value of such with the knowledge of costs and prices to do so it has been of much benefit to many buyers and investors. Developing and planning improvements on different properties is not a one plan fits all.

Each property needs a plan of strategy to achieve the long , medium or short term goal in capital spending versus capital gain and gain on investment.

We do consulting for landowners on their long term planning and distribution once we know their plans and goals that they would like to achieve and how to best get it accomplished.

We also do consultation and management for absentee owners on a month to month basis as per their requirement.

The consultation also pertains to people who are looking to buy hunting land, recreational land, productive farming land which could be leased out to create income, CRP program land which is earning by way of Government payments and land bought for long term investments. Many times it is just the plain cattle ranches that are the long term stable investment properties that are the foundation of portfolio building. Those cattle ranches are the ones when leased to cattle men, which are the best looked after over a long period of time and people who have a interest in the land and do not simply do any changes by way of roads, ponds, clearings and buildings which is not in the best interest of the land.

Through our vast knowledge of Texas we are able to advise buyers and investors on land and properties in such a way without having any bias to any one area or location.

Many buyers or sellers agents do not always exhibit the best interest for their clients in advising them on very important issues and a small number truly have the farming and ranching background experience of the industry before they became real estate agents. Having been in management of large farming and ranching operations for 18 years has truly put us in a position of knowledge and expertise when it does come to advising land investors on what they are looking at, knowing what their goals are.

We have had the privilege of working very close with one investor and have bought and sold many properties for him and can confidently report that over time that it was a very financial benefit for him. We also undertook the oversight of the improvements on the properties on his behalf.

With the ease of looking up everything on the internet these days most people think that they immediately know all of how to do stuff especially when it comes to unregulated things as owning land and buying it since they are financially able to do so, go out and doing some improvements and then find out that they made some mistakes along the way in not doing the needed research or having the knowledge of what they were doing. Being able to pay for it does not always make you a professional at what you are busy with.

It is with much respect to the land and the creation of it, that we offer our services in consultation on any issues pertaining to purchasing, selling and improvement and management of land to you as a buyer, seller or investor.

As the slogan goes that everything must be "Politically Correct " that in that light we offer you our services that we always undertake to be "Truthfully Correct" in all we do and say.

Solar Projects.

In addition to Recreational, Farming and Ranch land, USA Land and Ranch Realty is looking for investors and/or developers who are interested in solar farm projects. We can procure land for you by way of either purchase or leasing.
