Buyer's Guide

Our team is ready to guide and help you through the buying process. We are committed to courteous, professional and expedient service to help you. Our licensed agents specialize in Texas real estate and will find you the right property at the best price.

Seller's Guide

Selling your Property

Dirk van Reenen Sr. of USA Land and Ranch is a local real estate expert ready to help you sell your land or property.

From setting the price to creating marketing traditional and the Web, we have a plan that will match your land with the right buyers at the right price. We are committed to courteous, professional and fast personal service to help you feel at ease throughout the selling process.

From any small acreage to very large tracts of land we service the entire market ,hunting, recreational, cattle and farming, irrigation, oil and gas, solar, wind and also investment buyers. We reach many people by personal contact who have been of our clients before and who have contacted us in the past looking for a specific property. We have an extensive customer base old and new seeking property.

Each person is proud of what they own and would earnestly like to showcase their unique property to the best possible extreme and earn the best possible price. That is where we can be of extreme service to you the seller because we have the same values as you do about your property and no property is less important to us than any other. Each property is equally important because we are dealing with you and your feelings and emotions when selling a property.

We, as stewards of the land, always make it our priority to conduct deals in the best interest of our clients and to the honor of the profession of being a real estate agent.
