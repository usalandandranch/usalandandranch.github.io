Dirk van Reenen Sr.

Dirk is the owner and founder of USA Land and Ranch, LLC of Amarillo, TX. He is the supervising agent company and manages several other entrepreneurial ventures. Dirk is also owner of the recently acquired Caprock Auction Group, headquartered in Amarillo, TX.

After completion of Agricultural school and military duty in South Africa Dirk went and worked on a kibbutz in Israel at Ayellet Hashagar. He travelled Europe and briefly worked in England then worked in Minnesota, US. Returning to SA, Dirk farmed and ranched for 20 years before relocating to Texas.

Dirk managed a farming operation, fertilizer and chemical application business, and grain elevator for four years at which time he then became the General Manager to Bowen Ranches of Texas for 14 years.

During his time in this position it was one of his functions to locate and purchase good properties with potential and then remodel the properties for full production before sale.

Dirk began working with Caprock Realty of Amarillo in 2013 and currently broker with Paul Harmon of Lubbock, TX.

Kim Kraaier

Kim is the Business Manager of USA Land and Ranch, LLC and the newly acquired Caprock Auction Group. Her professional experience has included working for Fortune 500 enterprises, start-up companies, public and private entities and everything in between. Her expertise lies in business and operations management with a focus in accounting, finance, compliance, systems implementation, software migration, process improvement and business growth acceleration.

Kim has been in the Amarillo area for three years, originally hailing from Colorado. Her passion lies in lifelong learning and continuous self-improvement. To date, she has received an associate's degree, two bachelor's degrees, a graduate certificate, and a masters degree. She is currently pursuing a PhD in addition to studying for the Texas Real Estate exam, which she will be taking in early 2019. Once licensed, she will take on the task of growing USA Land and Ranch's client base and extending the company's reach into the residential sector.

Striving for a positive attitude is key to every success in life. Kim's goal is to impact companies and individuals alike by helping them make the most out of their circumstances. Regardless of the industry, people can always use positivity in their work and their lives.
