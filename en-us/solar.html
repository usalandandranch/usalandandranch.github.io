Solar Projects

In addition to Recreational, Farming and Ranch land, USA Land and Ranch Realty is looking for investors and/or developers who are interested in solar farm projects. We can procure land for you by way of either purchase or leasing.

Having an agricultural background, Dirk van Reenen is able to find common ground and acceptance more easily with farms and ranchers and facilitate negotiations with them at terms and conditions beneficial to all parties. His agricultural background helps him foster understand and trust with land owners.

Dirk recently successfully negotiated and obtained the executive rights of the minerals for a solar farm project consisting of 1,000 acres of land in the rolling hills of Texas. Not all of the mineral rights owners were surface owners, adding to the complexity of the project. However, Dirk's knowledge and expertise resulted in the procurement of the land for the developer.
