Marketing & Auction Associate I/II


THE OPPORTUNITY: We are seeking a Marketing & Auction Associate I/II to join our growing team. This is an entry-level position and will take on the responsibility of marketing on social media platforms, print, digital and other mediums for the group of companies in addition to growing the existing auction business. This is a customer/client-facing role and incumbent must be comfortable working with the public, including soliciting auction business from local individuals, estates, and businesses.

THE COMPANY: USA Land and Ranch, LLC and its subsidiary company, Caprock Auction Group, comprise a local real estate brokerage and auction house. This group of companies was founded in 2013 by a single individual and is quickly growing in a dynamic team environment. Its small size provides a number of benefits, including one-on-one focus and training, flexibility, excellent customer service, and plenty of room for growth, development and role expansion.

THE MARKET: Amarillo, TX; Texas Panhandle and surrounding areas

THE IDEAL CANDIDATE: Bright, ambitious, hard-working, driven to succeed, passionate about helping others, must have an entrepreneurial spirit, friendly, team-player and customer-focused.

THE QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Required:
    • 18+ years of age
    • Clean driving record
    • Insurable
    • High School graduate/GED
    • Cash handling experience
    • Willingness/ability to travel locally
  • Preferred:
    • Some college and/or applicable experience
  • Strongly Preferred
    • Marketing experience
    • Social Media experience
    • Auction experience/knowledge

    THE REQUIREMENTS:

  • Able to learn/Trainable/Coachable.
  • Highly computer savvy with significant experience in Microsoft Office Suite, Google G-Suite and all relevant social media platforms.
  • Capable of process conceptualization -- ability to quickly learn, adapt and apply new information.
  • Independent personality -- ability to work independently, as well as collaboratively with cross-functional teams.
  • Interpersonal skills -- excellent verbal communication skills.
  • Positive attitude -- we are looking for someone who cares about helping people and growing business.
  • Motivated candidate -- able to work in a changing environment.
  • Have a desire to grow and expand role to include oversight of others.
  • Highly organized and detail-oriented with the ability to prioritize and multi-task.
  • Comfortable working in a customer-facing environment.
  • Ability to routinely lift up to 50 pounds without assistance.
  • Must be able to perform repetitive sitting, bending, standing and reaching motions -- work may not always be in a termperature-controlled environment.

    • THE RESPONSIBILITIES - LEVEL I:

  • Monitor and report on campaign metrics across multiple channels, providing insight and recommendations based on data.
  • Measure, track and report campaign performance.
  • Execute marketing and branding activities, both digitally and in person.
  • Work directly with customers to drive business.
  • Coordinate campaign planning, deployment, execution, follow-up and reporting.
  • Cross-training for multiple roles throughout organization.
  • Support functional day to day operations of CRM (Customer Relationship Management) and Marketing Automation platforms to include maintenance, configuration and reporting.
  • Setup, catalog and inventory auction merchandise for live and online auctions.

    • THE RESPONSIBILITIES - LEVEL II:

  • All responsibilities for Level I plus the following -
  • Conduct IT functions for a small organization.
  • Assist with implementing operations integrations.
  • Display a high level of emotional intelligence.
  • Utilize business acument to help grow the organization.
  • Expand on current concept of role, increasing responsibility and reach.

    • THE BENEFITS: Paid holidats after 90-day evaluation period.

    THE COMPENSATION:

  • Level I: $9.00-$10.00/hour depending on experience.
  • Level II: Negotiable. Compensation according to experience and expertise to include - tech savvy, IT capabilities, business acument and willingness to expand role.
  • Plus profit-sharing incentives and room for expansion.
  • Successful candidate will undergo a 90-day evaluation period prior to being hired on full time.
