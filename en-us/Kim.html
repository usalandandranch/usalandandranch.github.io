Kim Kraaier

Director of Finance and Business Operations

Kim is the leader of the finance and operations sectors of USA Land and Ranch, LLC and the Caprock Auction Group. Her professional experience has included Fortune 500 enterprises, start-up companies, and public and private entities. Her expertise lies in business and operations management with a focus in accounting, finance, compliance, systems implementation, software migration, process improvement and business growth acceleration.

Kim has been in the Amarillo area for three years, originally hailing from Colorado. Her passion lies in lifelong learning and continuous self-improvement. To date, she has earned three undergraduate degrees and two graduate degrees. She is currently pursuing a PhD in Performance Psychology, in addition to studying for the Texas Real Estate exam, which she will be taking in 2019. Once licensed, she will take on the task of growing USA Land and Ranch's client base and extending the company's reach into the residential sector.

Striving for a positive attitude is key to every success in life. Kim's goal is to impact companies and individuals alike by helping them achieve their goals. Regardless of the industry, people can always use positivity in their work and their lives.
