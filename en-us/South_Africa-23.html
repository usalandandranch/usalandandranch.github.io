Game: Impala, Kudu and small game like Steen buck, Duiker, Bushbuck and Warthogs do occur here naturally. Bird enthusiasts can add a host of bushveld endemics to their list, including Crimson-breasted Shrike, Martial Eagle, Kori Bustard, white-fronted Bee-eater, and Giant Kingfisher etc.

Fencing: This farm is fully game fenced with a 2.4m high steel wire and 21 strands according to the requirements of the Department of Nature Conservation.

Typographic: This farm has it all – Water, bushveld plains, mountains and on the horizon is the portrait of the Springbokflats – a picture that changes as the season changes – fabulous!! Here at this farm where the most spectacular sunsets are experienced. The lower part of the farm is typical Sweetveld with lots of Acacia and Maroela trees where the mountain area is more Sourverld, yet a much larger selection of indigenousness trees.
