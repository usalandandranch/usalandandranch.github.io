Game Farm Detail: This is one Superb Game Farm.

Game Farm in extend of ±2706 acres: Situated in a malaria free area, this Jewel of a game farm is ecologically superb with a rich mix of bushveld attractive rock formation at the far back of the farm and which covers a mere 5% of the total surface of the farm while the rest is relative flat with beautiful plains. The entrance gate promise an enjoyable experience to come unto this game farm.

Roads: The farm roads give good access to all sections of the farm. The farm has several kilometres of roads plus plenty of additional trails which is set out for quad trails.

Game Fence: Fully game fenced accordingly to the requirements of the Dept. Nature Conservation. Game fences are regular patrolled by ground staff. Only a small area has no game fencing due to a servitude and sheer cliffs.

Water: There is 3 earth dams and a perennial sprout that runs through the farm and is ample water suitable for human and animal consumption - Fish Eagles regularly visits this dam. Water is also obtained from 2 boreholes and cement reservoir feeding game drinking troughs which are widely spread out on the farm.

Flora: There is a great variety grasses and trees. Trees are not only functional, but also aesthetically pleasing to the eye, as can be seen from pictures. A wide variety of indigenous trees cover these beautiful area forming a breathtaking tranquil landscape and being an important part of animal, bird and insect nutrition.

Fauna: The Farm is extremely well stocked but also well balanced with a large variety of game. This region, being the centre of the South African breeding heartbeat, and well known between local game breeders, has some of the largest game breeders in the province.
