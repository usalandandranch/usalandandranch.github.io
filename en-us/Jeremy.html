Jeremy Jones

Real Estate Agent, CPPM

Jeremy Jones is a licensed real estate agent and a certified professional property manager, CPPM. His major focus is working with investors to grow their real estate portfolio, property management, BPOs and Foreclosure consultation.

His interest in Real Estate started at an investment company in Dallas, Texas where he helped build a Real Estate software that helped investor’s find discounted properties. He took his newfound interest in real estate and worked as a leasing agent at a traditional apartment complex while attending college. Within a year he quickly excelled and became the assistant manager where he decided to obtain a real estate license to continue his education within the field. He worked with the development of an entire student housing start-up in a college town handling all accounting issues and working directly with the hiring and managing of local vendors, he managed over 18-staff members and collected tenant rent for an 800-plus property unit complex. His latest achievement is working as a mortgage consultant helping families avoid foreclosures and keeping families in their homes.

He is finishing up a degree in International Economics at Texas Tech University and he plans to continue growing his business in Texas and becoming one of the top real estate agents who has built a clientele made up of new real estate investors and new homeowners.

“It is time for younger families and younger real estate investor’s, to reap the benefits of real estate ownership that many others have benefited from for many years.”
