About Us

USA Land and Ranch, LLC is a realty and auction house based in Amarillo, TX and serves the surrounding Texas Panahandle. Our team of skilled professionals understand that searching for and finding the right property can be stressful. But the process has never been easier than with the assistance of our dedicated staff, user-friendly site, and our committment to make your real estate experience a comfortable one. USA Land and Ranch Realty is ready to assist you, whether buying or selling, with all of our resources to locate and identity the ideal property to match your needs. Or if selling, to get your property listed and in front of the right prospective buyers. Our staff will work hard to make this an enjoyable and memorable experience for you.

Our expert real estate agents will compare and complete the task for you with skilled knowledge and experience, while simultaneously in the event of investment buying offer management service after purchase.

Mission and Goals

We pledge to conduct business at the highest level of integrity, while employing creativity and determination to strive for client satisfaction.

To engage in ecologically sensible land sales and development practices while being economically responsible to investment buyers and landowners.

To earn every client and fellow land professional's respect and lifetime friendship.

To help landowners and investment buyers preserve agricultural and recreational land indefinitely.

To practice Real Estate with conscience to benefit the long term good of the public, clients, and their neighbors.

To preserve the quality of rural life in Texas by discouraging small tract subdivisions.

Services

Marketing Representation - We posess superior presentation and communication skills which allow us to help landowners reach maximum realistic value of their land being sold as whole or parcel.

Value Assessment - We monitor sales allowing me to accurately help landowners determine realistic and optimistic value on their land.

Buyer Representation - Our knowledge of Texas land qualifies us to represent individuals, families, or investment buyers in their search for properties best suited to their requirements.

Private Treaty Transactions - We can arrange confidential quick purchase of your land at a negotiated price.
