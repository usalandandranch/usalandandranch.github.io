About Us

Searching for and finding Recreational, Farming and Ranch land in Texas has never been as easy and rewarding as on the USA Land and Ranch Realty website. USA Land and Ranch Realty is ready to assist you the buyer with all our resources to locate and identify the perfect property that matches your requirement. We as professionals, will work hard to make this an enjoyable and memorable experience for you.

Our expert real estate agents will compare and complete the task for you with skilled knowledge and experience, while simultaneously in the event of investment buying offer management service after purchase.

Mission and Goals

We pledge to conduct business at the highest level of integrity, creativity, and determination - striving for customer satisfaction.

To engage in ecologically sensible land sales and development practices while being economically responsible to investment buyers and landowners.

To earn every customer and fellow land professional's respect and lifetime friendship.

To help landowners and investment buyers preserve agricultural and recreational land indefinitely.

To practice Real Estate with conscience to benefit the long term good of the public, customers, and their neighbors.

To preserve the quality of rural life in Texas by discouraging small tract subdivisions.

Meet our team.

Dirk van Reenen Sr.

After completion of Agricultural school and military duty in South Africa Dirk went and worked on a kibbutz in Israel at Ayellet Hashagar. He travelled Europe and briefly worked in England then worked in Minnesota, US. Returning to SA, Dirk farmed and ranched for 20 years before relocating to Texas.

Dirk managed a farming operation, fertilizer and chemical application business, and grain elevator for four years at which time he then became the General Manager to Bowen Ranches of Texas for 14 years.

During his time in this position it was one of his functions to locate and purchase good properties with potential and then remodel the properties for full production before sale.

Dirk began working with Caprock Realty of Amarillo in 2013 and currently broker with AG Broker Ltd in Amarillo, Texas.
